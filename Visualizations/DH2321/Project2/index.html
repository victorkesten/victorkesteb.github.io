Learning


This project has taught me how to effectively visualize multiple variables. The project allows the user to brush on the parallel coordinate graph, organize the information by dragging the axis around and to get details on demand by clicking on names on the list by picking a category, hovering on the donut graph. The countries were picked specifically for their geographical locations, picking countries all over the world and the variables were picked in accordance to the research theme - Happiness. Unfortunately some countries did not have data for every year, and some variables differ (see 2000-2004). However - 30 countries, 20 years and 8+ variables were used.
