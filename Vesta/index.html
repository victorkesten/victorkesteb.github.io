ABOUT

Our name "Vesta" is inspired by the Roman Goddess of home and family. We aim to emulate her strength and grace by providing our clients with the very best service through hard work, professionalism and integrity.

Ohana Means Family


Started by Courtney and Ted Takai, Vesta Hawaii Real Estate is a new venture that has resulted from years of experience in the Hawaii real estate market, and a worthwhile career move. Together with their small team of professionals, they exceed expectations, and deliver at consistently high standards - in short, they give nothing less than you deserve.

Being a locally owned business, Vesta specializes in Hawaii real estate, specifically on the island of Oahu. However, the Vesta team has had much experience working with clients from all around the world, and they always look forward to the opportunity to share the secrets and stories of their beautiful island.

BUY

Helping Kama'aina Find Their Way Home

The comittment to buying a house can be both the most exciting, and most daunting, decision of your life. Whether you're a first-time homebuyer or a seasoned investor, we will assist you through the many steps of buying by finding a home that you will love, guiding you through the process with care, and ensuring you the best transaction possible.

With our advanced search engine, you will be able to view the newest and most sought-after properties on the island.

Here are just a few of the things we will do to help you find the home of your dreams:
  • Discuss your wants and needs
  • Select properties of interest and schedule showings
  • Accompany you on visits and listen to your critiques
  • Help you get pre-qualified and pre-approved for loans
  • Write purchase offers and cover letters to present to listing agents
  • Manage the entire escrow process from start to finish

Contact us for a buyers information packet!

SELL

Get it listed. Get it sold. Get happy.


Are you looking to sell? Perhaps you need more space for a growing family, or are simply taking a new job elsewhere. Whatever the reason, let us help you in the mission to sell your home!

If you decide to work with Vesta, we guarantee that your transaction will be smoother and simpler than you ever thought it could be. Our dedicated team of Realtors along with our extensive list of hard-working vendors - and our knowledge of the current market - will help you get the most out of your sale, and alleviate any stress along the way.

Here are just a few of the things that we will do to ensure your home goes onto the market with a bang:
  • Go over comparable properties to determine a fair market value
  • Discuss ways to maximize your profits
  • Stage your homes using stylish, eye catching decor
  • Have professional photographs taken of your home
  • Market your listing to reach the widest possible audience
  • Advertise on our multiple social media sites
  • Show your home, both privately and for open houses
  • Manage the escrow from start to finish
For more information on selling, please get in touch and we'll send you our Listing Information Packet!
