Who am I?

My name is Victor Kesten and I have a Master of Science in Engineering in Computer Science (Civilingenjörs* inom Datateknik) from Kungliga Tekniska Höskolan in Stockholm, Sweden. Currently, while simultaneously working fulltime creating data visualization tools, I am pursuing a degree in Political Science from Stockholm University. At an early age I moved from Sweden to Beijing, China and so I've spent most of my life living abroad, learning about foreign cultures and connecting with people all over the world. My true passions are visualizaing information and doing data analysis, long distance running and exploring the chemistry behind brewing beer.
* Civilingenjör is equivalent to a Master of Science degree in Engineering.
