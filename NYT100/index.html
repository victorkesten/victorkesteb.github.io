New Year’s Eve. A song. A conversation. A month later and nothing was more clear than that this was ‘it’. We both knew so without a doubt. And what is amazing - even to this day, is that it really was true. It is true. But the timing was not right. We weren’t ready. And you couldn’t wait. You wouldn’t wait. And now that we are ready - now that everything we planned can happen, you’ve vanished into thin air and left me behind without a care. But I know you still have the compass. And you know how to find your way home. I know you do. I’ll always be your home. Your rock.
