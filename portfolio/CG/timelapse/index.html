Timelapse


Timelapse was a collaborative project in which our goal was to design and create a vertical slice, or demo, of a game. The goal was to experience the entire production cycle of a larger project, working through every step of the game design pipeline. Together as a group we unanimously decided to revive a lost genre - Rhythm based games. Timelapse takes the best of rhythm based games and combines them with a top-down view bullet hell shooter. The project was created using Unity and all assets are original. The goal of our demo was to create something of very high quality while also providing a fun and complete game experience.



My role programming wise within this project was to design the character system and gameplay programming elements. This involved a lot of C# development and utilizing Unity's functionality to its max. Beyond this I was responsible for market research, analysis and website creation/monitoring.
