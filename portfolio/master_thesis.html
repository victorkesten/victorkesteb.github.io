Master Thesis


"What are the main affordances and limitations of supporting the principal tasks of artisanal beer brews in understanding and tracking their own creations and that of their community of practice when using information visualization tools as measured by power to inform reproducibility of results in brewing, discovery of flavor and texture patterns, recipe recall and recipe creation/fine tuning?"


In support of the investigation into this research question I have designed and created a general-purpose visualization system for a brewers database that tracks and visualizes things such as batch logs, recipes and fermentation/mash schedules as well as overall insight into current brewery production. The work was done primarily together with Sigtuna Brygghus in Stockholm, Sweden and also with help from multiple breweries across the world.

A link to the actual project itself can be provided upon request. Due to secrecy policies the data used for the visualization cannot be shared online and only a mock version of said project can be displayed.
